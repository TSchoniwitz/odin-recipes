Oven Roasted Chicken Thighs

Description

This is a recipe for oven roasted chicken thighs. We'll be using bone-in skin-on chicken thighs here. This results in meat that is tender and juicy, and crispy skin.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs chicken thighs, bone-in skin-on
  • 2 Tbsp oil
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp onions powder
  • 2 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Line a large baking sheet with foil and set aside.
  2. Remove chicken thighs from packaging and pat dry. Place them on baking sheet and rub down with the oil.
  3. In a small bowl, mix the spices and seasoning together. Evenly sprinkle over the chicken, make sure to get both sides of chicken.
  4. Bake chicken in oven for 35-45 minutes, or until fully cooked.
  5. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve.
