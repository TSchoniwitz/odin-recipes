Slow Cooker Mozzarealla Stuffed Meatballs

Description

This is a recipe for mozzarella stuffed meatballs made in a slow cooker. Making it in a slow cooker helps to keep the meatballs very juicy. The meatballs are good on their own, or you can cook some pasta to go along with it.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb italian sausage
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/3 onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 mozzarella sticks, cut into 4-5 even pieces
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup parsley
  • Canned marinara sauce

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine all ingredients except mozzarella sticks and marinara sauce.
  2. Take a golf ball sized piece of the meat mixture, and press a mozzarella piece into the center. Form the meat around the mozzarella, fully enclosing it. Repeat until all the meat mixture is used.
  3. Place meatballs into slow cooker and cover with marinara sauce.
  4. Cover and cook on high for 2-2 1/2 hours, or until meat is fully cooked.
  5. Serve by itself, or with pasta.
